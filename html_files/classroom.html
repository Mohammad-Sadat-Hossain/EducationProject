Meet Our Dynamic Team

English Learning Platform (ELP) by Education Center

About Us

ELP by Education Center was founded in the year 2008 under the name of Innovative English Learning Centre (IELC) by our Managing Director Mr. Abbas Karimi. At the time, Chittagong was faced with a dearth of qualified teachers capable of training students as per the required international standards of the IELTS exam. Since its inception, it has been the mission of our establishment to enhance the English Language proficiency and communication skills of all our participating students. ELP has evolved with the technological advancements of the present day to become Bangladesh’s first and only English Learning Centre to use a digital self- study app. It is our founder’s vision and ELP’s main aim to revolutionize the English Language learning process in the country by using innovative and updated teaching techniques that cater specifically to the needs of the Bangladeshi students. Over the years we have had more than 1000 students who have successfully completed their IELTS Examination and various other English language courses under us. Following our profound rate of success, we have also been enlisted by the British Council Bangladesh as a Gold partner and an official registration point for IELTS.

What We Do

ELP offers the perfect blend of technology and human touch to enhance the students’ learning experience. We use diversified forms of teaching methods that are marked with creativity and cater to the unique needs of our students. We take pride and pleasure in serving our students with our years of professional expertise. Our qualified and trained team are continuously developing their skills and knowledge in order to help and guide students to the achievement of their lifelong goals

Digital Study Platform, Physical and Zoom Classes

We have made our courses more accessible than ever before by operating classes both online and offline, all seven days of the week. Our classes follow the hybrid system where students can either choose to study on location or via zoom at the comfort of their own home. Our digital self-study app allows students to have access to all relevant class material without having to always be physically present. We provide a free trial version of this app for 3 days for those who are interested in purchasing it. This comprehensive app allows students to study English in a intriguing manner at any time and at any place.
