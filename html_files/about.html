About Us

Education Center is a professional International education and immigration consultant based in Chittagong, Bangladesh. We are the first ICEF certified agent in Chittagong and one of the best education service provider in town. Education Center is a sister concern of IELC (Innovative English Learning Center). The inception of IELC is dated back in Feb 2008. Both Education Center and IELC are enterprises that are on the same premises in Chittagong. IELC has rebranded under the Education Center Ltd name as Education Center English Learning Platform. The official inauguration of Education Center was on the 1st of February 2010. We have our subsidiary operational branch in Banani, Dhaka since 2019.

OUR VISION

Being aware of the current tendency of students to go abroad to seek quality education, our vision is to become the household name in the global education industry in terms of transparency & efficiency. We strive to perform diligently to be recognised as the best service provider with the highest quality, standards, and values in support of our clientele.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to assist Bangladeshi students to get admission in top educational institutions worldwide by providing a professional service in selecting the most appropriate course and institution for a better and brighter future.
